About Us!

We Are Leader In Pharmaceuticals

Eldobase Chemists LTD stands as the premier pharmaceutical establishment in the Northrift and Western Regions of Kenya, dedicated to enhancing the health and well-being of the community. Our strategic base of operations is located in Eldoret Town, a pivotal Regional Medical Hub that proudly accommodates the second-largest Referral Hospital in the entirety of Kenya.

With a commitment to superior service, we have strategically positioned our wholesale and retail outlets in key locations, including Eldoret, Kitale and Kapsabet towns. These branches ensure that our pharmaceutical expertise and products are readily accessible to a wider demographic.

As a trusted pharmacy, Eldobase Chemists LTD serves as a distinguished agent representing numerous multinational pharmaceutical corporations and local manufacturers. This position empowers us to guarantee the availability of genuine, high-quality medicines to our esteemed clientele at affordable prices, epitomizing our mission to make healthcare accessible to all.

Eldobase Chemists LTD prides itself on maintaining a team of skilled professionals who operate with the utmost professionalism. This team of experts diligently caters to the diverse needs of our clients, fostering a culture of excellence in every facet of our services. Our commitment to professionalism extends to our robust collaboration with a wide spectrum of healthcare institutions, including government hospitals, faith-based healthcare facilities, private clinics, and pharmacies.

At Eldobase Chemists LTD, we hold a steadfast reputation for adhering to the highest standards of pharmaceutical care. As a testament to our dedication to patient safety and optimal health outcomes, we unwaveringly respect and honor every doctor's prescription, refraining from any practice of substituting medications without explicit consent.

In essence, Eldobase Chemists LTD transcends the conventional boundaries of a mere pharmacy. We stand as a beacon of pharmaceutical excellence, committed to enhancing the health and quality of life for individuals and communities throughout the Northrift and Western Regions of Kenya.
